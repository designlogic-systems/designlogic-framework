## Role In The Framework

Review workflows are applied DesignLogic Framework procedures for inspecting bounded artifacts, claims, transitions, handoffs, or proposed uses before downstream reliance.

A review workflow may compose existing framework surfaces such as lenses, artifact contracts, authority boundaries, handoff boundaries, and verification support.

A review workflow does not establish implementation, product validation, deployment readiness, production readiness, customer acceptance, market validation, or authority to act.
